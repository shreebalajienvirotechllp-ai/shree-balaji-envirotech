## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Business not showing in Maps
**Solution:**
1. Ensure profile is 100% complete
2. Verify business address
3. Add more photos (minimum 10)
4. Get 5+ reviews
5. Post regularly (2-3x per week)
6. Wait 2-4 weeks for indexing

### Issue: Low visibility
**Solution:**
1. Optimize business description with keywords
2. Add all relevant categories
3. Upload high-quality photos
4. Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
5. Post 3x per week minimum
6. Get more recent reviews
7. Enable all features (messaging, booking, etc.)

### Issue: Not appearing for "near me" searches
**Solution:**
1. Verify your exact address in GBP
2. Add service areas
3. Use location keywords in description
4. Ensure NAP consistency across all directories
5. Build local citations
6. Get reviews from local customers

### Issue: Duplicate listings
**Solution:**
1. Search for your business name + city
2. Report duplicates via GBP dashboard
3. Mark the correct listing as primary
4. Google will merge after verification (2-3 weeks)
